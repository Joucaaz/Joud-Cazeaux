GAME4J

×
The goal of the game is to get to the house at the bottom right without walk on an obstacles (trees, rocks) that make you lose 10 point life and the bonuses (meat, fruits) make you gain 10 life.

Different features: see the best path in terms of distance and energy (Dijkstra), save and resume a game later, see the replay of won games, see the loop when passing on a square already visited.

This work was done as part of a project in the first year of engineering school at CY Tech (ex-EISTI).
